Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our events and services

How do I register for upcoming events?

You can register for our events through our website by clicking the 'Register Now' button on any event card. Early bird discounts are available for most events.

What industries do you serve?

We specialize in International Education, Information Technology, Teaching & Learning, and Not-for-Profit sectors, bringing together professionals from these key industries.

Do you offer virtual event options?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual participation options for most of our events, ensuring global accessibility for all professionals.

What micro-credentials do you provide?

We offer industry-recognized certifications in areas such as international education management, digital transformation, and professional development leadership.

About Corpgems

Corpgems orchestrates world-class networking events across New Zealand, Australia, Canada, the United States, the UK, Finland, Japan, and the Philippines. We bring together visionary professionals from International Education, IT, Teaching & Learning, and Not-for-Profit sectors to share knowledge, forge partnerships, and drive industry innovation.

Our mission is to create meaningful connections that transcend geographical boundaries, empowering professionals to Learn, Network, and Grow together. Through our premium events, micro-credentials, and development programs, we're building a global community of changemakers who shape the future of their industries.
